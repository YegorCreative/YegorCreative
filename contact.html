Project Checklist

To get started quickly, it helps if you can share:

  • Business Name – What should I call your business or project?
  • Goal – What should the site achieve? (e.g., more leads, online bookings, brand awareness)
  • Pages/Sections – What content do you need? (Home, About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Examples – Any websites you admire or want to emulate?
  • Deadline – When do you need it live?
  • Budget Range – Knowing your budget helps me recommend the right solution.
  • Content Readiness – Do you have logos, photos, and text ready, or need help creating them?

Don't worry if you don't have all of this yet—we can figure it out together.
